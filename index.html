ABOUT

         Captain Masr Imaginary Universe (CMIU) was an idea came that by chance to Ziad Salah's mind. The idea was promoted by Adam Rohayem forming the first movie of Captain Masr. At first, most characters and relationships were inspired from Ziad's personal life. Later, the characters' personalities became independent of their real-life counterparts. The story was promoted and encouraged later by Omar Mohamed. This encouraged Ziad to create two sequels for the story. Finally, the story being liked by several people (including Miss Egypt) made the "Captain Masr Imaginary Universe" be adopted by Zezoosalah 89 Production team, producing more movies to this fictional universe.
