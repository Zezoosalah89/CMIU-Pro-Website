Bermuda's Vision

  • Name: Jarvis
  • Type: Super Intelligent Robot
  • Universe: U001
  • Role: Leader of the Ultrons

 Jarvis was created by Ziad and Yassin as a member of the EGYknights. Later, Bermuda's conscious infected the Ultrons and chose Jarvis as its main mind. He became Bermuda's Vision. His agenda is to rule the world with fear to force his fake justice.


 - Character played by Bryan Dechart

Powers

  • Telekinesis
  • Nano Technology
  • Gamma Radiation
  • Situation Analysis

Weapon
