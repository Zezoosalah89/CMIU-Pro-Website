The Móshù shī fâting 魔術師法庭

  • Founder: Qiāng Tāo
  • Type: Religous Cult
  • Nation: International
  • Role: Supervillains

After Shīfâ zhē was trapped in the Quantum Realm, a chinese religous sect found him and made him a statue, from which he could communicate with them. The Móshù shī fâting worshipped Shīfâ zhē and called him by this name, he gave them super powers in return. The goal of the Móshù shī fâting is to find and eliminate the Aqualiens , so that Shīfâ zhē can get out of the Quantum Realm and be in his true cosmic form and proceed in invading the universe. He promises them with their own universe after that.
