The Aqualiens

The Aqualiens are organisms living in a planet in a galaxy far away from Earth called Aquarium.


- Fictional creatures created and designed by Ziad Salah

Powers

  • Superhuman Strength & Duriability
  • Fit movement (males)
  • Super mental power , sence , future glimpses
  • Health Regeneration
  • Underwater Breathing
  • Resistance to water pressure
  • Energy Projection
  • Teleportation portals
  • Water Manipulation
  • Mythical Form

Planet Aquarium


Planet Aquarium is all water with no land , with rocks , plants and sea creatures in the depth. The volume of the planet is relatively small , as it is smaller than the Earth's moon.

Appearance

They have a wide black eye in their heads , an organ on the forehead used for hearing and talking , nostrils at the shoulders (they can breathe in & outside water). Their mouth is located in their chest just used to eat and to defend. Finaly , they secrete their wastes from their skin The male has feet with claws and a swimming tail full of thorns , and two deadly torns at the end of the tail . He does not have arms but has tentacles in his head used for attacking and constipation of things. In addition to two fins under the shoulder f or swimming . He has pollen chambers on his head to spread a fluid filled with pollen grains for reproduction. The female doesn't have legs , but has a tail such as a mermaid , full of thorns with two deadly thorns at the end. She has thin arms with deadly claws and fins under its arms . She has a long Gynoecium in her head used in attacks and reception of pollen grains to form seeds and plant her children in rocks , soon the plant gives fruit ( like an egg ) , which hatches giving birth to a baby aqualien.

Lifestyle

Just like bees and ants , there is a queen who is served by all the others , the queen is much larger in size , and she plants plenty of eggs to secure the continuity of the species. When they die , they transform into light with no remaining body , they can choose to transfer their powers to someone (mythical transference , leads to instant death).

Weakness

The Mythril Pealm

It is a special rock in Aquarium that disables some of the Aqualien's powers (regeneration, energy projection, mythical form, portals, water manipulation).
