Gamora

  • Full Name: Gamora Zen Whoberi Ben Titan
  • Type: Alien (Zen-Whoberis)
  • Gender: Female
  • Birthdate: Unknown
  • Nationality: Zen-Whoberi
  • Role: Guardian of the Galaxy

Gamora, known as the deadliest woman in the galaxy, is a highly skilled warrior and a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy. She is the adopted daughter of Thanos, but she turned against him to fight for justice.

Portrayed by Zoe Saldana, Gamora is a beloved character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Powers

  • Super Strength
  • Enhanced Agility
  • Skilled Combatant
  • Healing Factor

Gadgets

Godslayer Sword

Planet: Zen-Whoberi

Zen-Whoberi is a planet that was once a peaceful paradise, home to the Zen-Whoberis. However, it was ravaged by Thanos, leaving few survivors, including Gamora.

Appearance

Gamora is a green-skinned alien with distinct facial markings. She has a tall, athletic build and vibrant green skin, which sets her apart from most beings in the galaxy.

Lifestyle

She maintains a disciplined lifestyle, constantly honing her combat skills and staying physically fit. Gamora's lifestyle reflects her warrior spirit, driven by a desire to protect the galaxy.

Weakness

Weapon Name: Soul Stone

The Soul Stone is one of the few things capable of truly harming Gamora, as it holds immense power and can manipulate souls.
