Shīfâ zhē 施法者

  • Name: (unknown)
  • Type: Lexion
  • Gender: (unknown)
  • Size: indefinite
  • Birthdate: (unknown)
  • Role: Cosmic Threat

He is the one who remained from his people, who were livng on a planet called Lexus. Every lexion was born with a special power, Shīfâ zhē got the power of consuming other's. He absorbs the strength and the spirits of other organisms. This makes him grow stronger and live longer. Shīfâ zhē has killed all of his people and absorbed the biomass of his planet. He did that to other planets and was known to be the deadly conquerer of the galaxies.

Planet Lexus

Shīfâ zhē was cursed and trapped in the Quantum Realm in a rock (which is later converted to a statue) by the Aqualien Queen on Earth. The only way to break the curse is to kill all her offsprings. Later, the statue was found by the Móshù shī fâting.
